Work History

I have worked trades most of my adult life and have done many different types of jobs that I have enjoyed and done well in. I started out working at Mercy Hospital in Springfield for my first big boy job in their environmental services department where I did janitorial work like buffing and laying wax. From there I got into the maintenance department where I learned many different skills like painting, drywall, flooring, construction, vinyl installation and even graphic design. I have also worked in a welding shop for 3 years as a lead in the Electropolish department, worked as a grinder, tried my hand at welding, and manned the waterjet. I have returned to work at Mercy where I am currently at while I go to school for my AAS.CIS in Computer Information Science where I want to pursue a career in either program development or cyber security. I still haven't stuck with what I want to be when I grow up.
